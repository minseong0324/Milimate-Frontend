## Project Introduction

As the Internet letter printing service in the training center has been suspended since 2023.08.14, the training center's acquaintances answer random questions every day and help you cheer up during the difficult training center by allowing you to know messages of support and your usual thoughts about the training center. You can get answers from acquaintances by sharing your question link on SNS. Trainees' acquaintances can choose the color of the letter, and for lower entry hurdles, no login is required for the trainer's acquaintance to leave an answer. The trainee's facial expressions are also presented randomly. The trainer's questions will be updated as of 17:30 every day, and answers will be available (17:30 = personal maintenance time starts). We expected that acquaintances who write answers to trainees will become the main user base, so we organized the UI with a cuter image.
